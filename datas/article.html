Functional Skincare + Light Protection Are Non-Negotiable!

After applying functional skincare products, the most important step in your routine is protecting your skin from UV exposure. High-performance active ingredients can become unstable when exposed to sunlight, and may even trigger side effects such as hyperpigmentation or inflammation. That’s why diligent sun protection is essential. On days you use functional actives, apply a generous amount of sunscreen at least two hours before going out. Ingredients like retinol or AHA, which increase photosensitivity, are best reserved for nighttime use.

For sensitive skin, what matters most isn’t just what you use, but how you use it. Especially with functional skincare, following the correct order—and always including calming and barrier-repair steps in your routine—is key to achieving both effectiveness and safety.
